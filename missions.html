Mission Planning Process

01

Intel Gathering

Collect and analyze mission intelligence including maps, enemy positions, and objectives.

02

Team Assembly

Select appropriate team members based on mission requirements and individual specialties.

03

Loadout Selection

Choose equipment and weapons based on mission type, environment, and team roles.

04

Strategy Development

Develop tactical approach including entry points, communication protocols, and contingency plans.

05

Briefing

Team briefing covering objectives, roles, communication signals, and emergency procedures.

06

Execution

Mission execution with real-time adaptation to changing circumstances and enemy responses.
