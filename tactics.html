Communication Protocols

Radio Procedures

Call Signs

  • Alpha 1 - Team Leader
  • Bravo 1 - Second in Command
  • Charlie 1 - Sniper
  • Delta 1 - Medic

Standard Phrases

  • "Contact" - Enemy spotted
  • "Moving" - Team is advancing
  • "Cover" - Taking cover
  • "Clear" - Area secured

Hand Signals

Movement Signals

  • Fist - Stop
  • Open Hand - Move forward
  • Point - Direction to move
  • Thumbs Up - All clear

Combat Signals

  • Finger to Eye - Enemy spotted
  • Hand to Ear - Listen
  • Finger to Lips - Quiet
  • Hand to Throat - Cut comms
